Enhanced DID & VC Trust Flow

Deep dive into DID Generate, Publish, Resolve, and VC Verification.

1. DID GENERATE & Publish

The University creates keys and publishes its DID Document to the Registry.

2. VC Issue & Sign

The University signs the data using its Private Key to create a VC (Digital Diploma).

3. VC Present & DID RESOLVE

The Student shares the VC. The Verifier (Job Site) starts DID Resolution.

4. VC Verify

The Verifier checks the VC Signature against the Public Key from the DID Document.
